Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

This policy explains what personal data LotteryPro collects, why, and your rights. We are the data controller for your account information.

1. Data we collect

  • Account data: your name and email address, provided when you register.
  • Usage data: basic technical information needed to run the Service (for example, your saved games and preferences).
  • Payment data: handled entirely by our payment provider, Paddle. We do not see or store your full card details.

2. How we use your data

To create and manage your account, provide the Service and its features, process your subscription, and contact you about your account.

3. Legal basis (UK/EU GDPR)

We process your data to perform our contract with you (providing the Service), and on the basis of our legitimate interests in operating and securing the Service.

4. Service providers we use

We share the minimum data needed with trusted providers who process data on our behalf:

  • Supabase — secure account and database hosting.
  • Paddle — payment processing and Merchant of Record.
  • Vercel and Railway — website and server hosting.
  • A licensed lottery results data provider (results only; no personal data shared).

5. Cookies and local storage

We use only the storage strictly necessary to keep you signed in and remember your preferences. We do not use advertising trackers.

6. Data retention

We keep your account data while your account is active. If you delete your account, we remove your personal data, except where we must keep records for legal reasons.

7. Your rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data, to object to or restrict processing, and to data portability. To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below.

8. International transfers

Some providers may process data outside your country. Where they do, appropriate safeguards are in place.
